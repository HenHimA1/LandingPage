Green Living in South Jakarta

South Jakarta is renowned for its vibrant lifestyle, culinary scene, and residential comfort. However, its true hidden gems are its public parks and green spaces. These "lungs of the city" provide a much-needed escape from the urban hustle, offering fresh air, community gathering spots, and safe environments for children and pets to play. Whether you are looking for a jogging track to maintain your fitness, a dog-friendly enclosure, or a quiet bench to read a book, South Jakarta has a park for you.

Our directory curates the best access points to nature in the Kemang, Pasar Minggu, and Jati Padang areas. We believe that access to green space is essential for mental well-being and community health. Explore our top picks below and start your journey to a greener lifestyle today.

Como Park

★ 4.6 (2,081 Reviews)

Located in the heart of Kemang, Como Park is the premier destination for dog lovers in Jakarta. It features a massive, enclosed grassy area where dogs can run off-leash safely. Beyond the park, the complex houses a variety of lifestyle businesses including a pizzeria, a coffee shop, and a gelateria, making it a perfect weekend hangout for the whole family.

Pejatian Park

★ 4.7 (350 Reviews)

Taman Pejatian is a serene urban oasis tucked away in West Pejaten. Known for its lush landscaping and towering shade trees, it offers a cool respite from the heat. The park features a well-maintained jogging track, reflexology paths, and ample seating areas. It is an ideal spot for morning exercise, meditation, or a quiet afternoon walk.

Taman Batan

★ 4.4 (164 Reviews)

Situated within a residential complex in Pasar Minggu, Taman Batan is a beloved community park. It offers a safe and friendly environment with a dedicated playground for children and open spaces for badminton. The park is known for its peaceful atmosphere and is a popular spot for neighbors to socialize in the evenings.

RPTRA Asoka

★ 4.5 (Community)

RPTRA (Ruang Publik Terpadu Ramah Anak) Asoka is a vibrant community hub in Jati Padang. Designed to be child-friendly, it hosts various free activities from futsal and traditional dance to early childhood education. It serves as a center for community development and social interaction for residents of all ages.
