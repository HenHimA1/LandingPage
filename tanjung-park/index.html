Taman Tanjung 2 might be compact, but it offers a mighty escape from the urban grind.

Why Visit?

In a city as bustling as Jakarta, finding a quiet spot to hear your own thoughts is a luxury. Taman Tanjung 2 provides exactly that. Located just off the main road, it serves as a green buffer, offering cleaner air and a cooler microclimate. It's an ideal spot to practice Mindfulness.

Local Guide

Taman Tanjung 2 is a favorite among local residents of Jagakarsa and office workers from nearby TB Simatupang buildings looking for a lunch-break escape. Although facilities are minimal compared to larger parks, its charm lies in its simplicity.
