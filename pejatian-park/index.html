Health & Wellness

Taman Pejatian is designed to promote a healthy lifestyle for all residents. The park's layout encourages physical activity through its thoughtfully designed infrastructure.

  • Jogging Track: The paved track loops around the park, offering a smooth surface for runners and walkers. One full loop is approximately 400 meters.
  • Reflexology Path: A dedicated stone path is available for foot reflexology, helping to improve circulation and reduce stress.
  • Clean Air: Surrounded by dense foliage, the park acts as a carbon sink, providing cleaner, fresher air compared to the roadside.
